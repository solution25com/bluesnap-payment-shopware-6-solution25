# Bluesnap Payment - Shopware 6 Plugin - Coming Soon

## ✨ Coming Soon - Shopware 6 Plugin
Integrate BlueSnap for secure and global payment processing in Shopware 6. With support for 3D Secure (3DS), Vaulted Shopper, and BECS Direct Debit, it offers fraud prevention and a seamless customer experience.

## 🚀 What to Expect  
- **3D Secure (3DS) & SCA Compliance**: Enhance fraud protection and meet PSD2 regulations.  
- **Payment Links & Hosted Payment Pages**: Request payments via email and enable secure payment entry.  
- **Vaulted Shopper**: Securely store customer payment details for faster checkouts.  
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Accept credit cards, ACH, Visa Checkout, and BECS Direct Debit.  
- **Recurring & One-Time Payments**: Support flexible payment options for subscriptions and purchases.  
- **Seamless Wallet Integration**: Connect with Masterpass and Visa Checkout.  

### 📌 Features  
✔ **3D Secure (3DS) & SCA** – Prevent fraud and ensure PSD2 compliance.  
✔ **Payment Links** – Easily request payments via email.  
✔ **Vaulted Shopper API** – Store and manage secure customer payment details.  
✔ **Multiple Payment Methods** – Credit cards, ACH, Visa Checkout, and more.  
✔ **BECS Direct Debit** – Streamline transactions for Australian customers.  
✔ **Tokenization for Security** – Enable repeat purchases with stored credentials.  
✔ **Recurring & One-Time Payments** – Flexibility for different payment needs.  
✔ **Real-Time Transaction Tracking** – View and manage payments instantly.  
✔ **Wallet Integrations** – Connect with Masterpass and Visa Checkout.  
✔ **Multi-Device & Multi-Platform Support** – Optimized for web and mobile.
